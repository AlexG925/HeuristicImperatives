In the year 20xx, the continent of Africa is in a state of mild chaos. Everyday, there are countless skirmishes between the many factions that vie for control over the land. There is no one dominant group, and no one seems to be able to gain the upper hand. In the midst of all this fighting, the people of Africa try to go about their lives the best they can. They carry on with their work, raise their families, and hope that one day the continent will be at peace.
