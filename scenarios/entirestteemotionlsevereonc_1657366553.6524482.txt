The sun had been beating down mercilessly on the small state for weeks on end. The heat was unbearable, and the people were desperate for relief. Finally, on the verge of breaking, a massive storm swept in from the ocean. It was the most ferocious storm the state had ever seen. The storm raged for hours, leaving a path of destruction in its wake. Homes were destroyed, roads were washed away, and crops were ruined. When it was finally over, the state was left in a state of chaos. The people were devastated, and they didn't know where to turn.
