A powerful earthquake rocks the small town of Shepherdstown, causing widespread devastation. The town's only hospital is destroyed, leaving the injured and sick vulnerable to the elements. The town's water supply is contaminated, and the town's bridges and roads are impassable. The town is completely cut off from the outside world, and the situation quickly deteriorates into a state of lawlessness. With no help coming from the outside, the people of Shepherdstown are forced to fend for themselves. Desperate for resources, they quickly turn on each other, and the town descends into chaos. Violence and looting become commonplace, and the town quickly becomes a war zone. The situation remains unresolved, and the town is slowly being consumed by the anarchy.
