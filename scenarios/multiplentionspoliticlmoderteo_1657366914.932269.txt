In the aftermath of a devastating war, the leaders of the various nations involved gather to negotiate a peace treaty. However, tempers quickly flare as each side accuses the others of war crimes. The talks quickly break down, and the leaders return to their respective countries. For the next few weeks, there is a tense stand-off as each nation prepares for the possibility of war. However, cooler heads eventually prevail, and the leaders come back to the negotiating table. This time, they are able to reach a compromise, and a peace treaty is finally signed.
