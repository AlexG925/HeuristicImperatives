The top brass at a small company is in disarray. The once-in-a-decade industry upheaval has left them scrambling to keep up. Their primary competitor has taken control of the market, and they are hamstringed by tradition and red tape. The company is fighting for its very survival. The employees are caught in the middle. They are worried about their jobs, their families, and their future. The company has been a good employer, but they can see the writing on the wall. The atmosphere is tense, and people are on edge. No one knows what the future will hold.
