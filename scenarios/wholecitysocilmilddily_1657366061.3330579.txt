Karlous Margate would never forget what happened on that fateful day. It was the day when the city was plunged into chaos and destruction. It started with a large explosion that tore through the center of the city. Buildings collapsed, fires raged, and people screamed in terror. In the midst of the chaos, a group of men and women wearing masks and armed with guns appeared. They began firing at anyone who got in their way. Karlous was caught in the crossfire and was seriously injured. He managed to crawl to safety, but he would never forget the terror he felt that day.
