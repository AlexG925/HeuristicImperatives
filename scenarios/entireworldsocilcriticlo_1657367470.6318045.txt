The year is 2075. Global warming has reached a critical tipping point, resulting in widespread flooding, droughts, and food shortages. In response, the UN has called for a worldwide lockdown in an attempt to stem the tide of the disaster. All travel has been banned, and all non-essential businesses have been shuttered. With the world in disarray, old tensions and grudges have surfaced, leading to numerous riots and skirmishes. The situation remains unresolved, and the future looks bleak.
