A sudden and severe storm lashes the city, knocking out power and communication lines. Public transportation grinds to a halt. Store shelves are quickly emptied as people stock up on supplies. Traffic lights go dark, leading to widespread gridlock. Emergency services are overwhelmed. The storm rages for days, leaving the city in darkness. People are trapped in their homes, unsure of what to do. Tempers flare and violence breaks out. Looting and rioting become common. The city descending into chaos. At the end of the storm, the city is left battered and broken. People are traumatized and many are left homeless. It will be years before the city recovers.
