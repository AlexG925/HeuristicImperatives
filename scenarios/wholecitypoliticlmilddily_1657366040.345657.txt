In the city of Kelmoir, the streets are abuzz with the latest news. It seems that the city's prized possessions, the magical artifacts known as the Kelmoir Stones, have been stolen! The city's guard is on high alert, searching for any clues as to who might have taken them. Meanwhile, the city's residents are in a panic, worried that the thief will be able to use the stones for evil purposes. No one knows what to do, and the situation remains unresolved.
