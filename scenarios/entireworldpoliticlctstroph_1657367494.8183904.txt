It was the year 20xx, and the world was in a state of panic. A massive pandemic had struck, killing millions and spreading terror and chaos in its wake. The governments of the world were desperately trying to contain the outbreak, but it was proving to be too much. With society crumbling and the death toll continuing to rise, it seemed like the end was near. Then, in a stroke of luck, a cure was found. But it was too late. The damage had been done. The world was a shadow of its former self, and the once-great nations were now struggling to survive. The pandemic had changed everything, and the world would never be the same again.
