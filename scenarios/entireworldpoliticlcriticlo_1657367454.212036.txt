In the year 2030, the world is on the brink of a political upheaval. Nearly every country is at odds with each other, and the UN is powerless to intervene. In the midst of this chaos, a new player emerges: the Global Liberation Army. The GLA is a terrorist organization that wants to overthrow the world order and establish a new world order. They have the backing of some of the world's most powerful countries, and they are not afraid to use violence to achieve their goals. The GLA is quickly gaining ground, and the world is in a state of panic. No one knows what to do, and the situation seems hopeless.
