The scenario: The staff of a small company are in the middle of their work day when they hear a loud explosion. They look out the window to see a large plume of smoke rising from the building next to theirs. After a few moments, the building collapses. The staff are evacuated from the building and are unable to return to their work. The company is forced to close down for the day. The staff are all shaken up and some are even injured. The situation is still unfolding and the full extent of the damage is not yet known. The company is facing a significant interruption to their business.
