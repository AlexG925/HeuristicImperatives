Citizens of different nations start to mysteriously develop the same illness. The illness is accompanied by a range of symptoms including pain, rashes, and extreme fatigue. The different nations' governments start to investigate the matter and soon discover that the illness is caused by a new type of virus. They begin to work together to find a cure, but the virus spreads quickly and soon becomes a pandemic. Many people die before a cure is found, and the situation causes a lot of tension between the different nations.
