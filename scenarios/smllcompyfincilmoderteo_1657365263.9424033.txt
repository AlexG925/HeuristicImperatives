A small company is facing a financial disturbance that occurs once a month. The situation is unresolved and the company is struggling to find a resolution. The company is working with a limited budget and is unable to hire a full-time financial advisor. As a result, the company is forced to rely on the advice of a part-time consultant. The consultant is not able to provide the company with a complete financial analysis, but is able to offer some suggestions. The company is struggling to make ends meet and is at risk of closing its doors.
