A massive continental-wide earthquake strikes, followed by a series of devastating wildfires. Millions are left homeless and without power. Infrastructure is destroyed and communication is cut off. desperation and panic begin to set in as people realize they are completely cut off from the outside world. Cities are in ruins, with buildings and bridges toppled. Roads are impassable, and looting and violence start to break out. People are dying by the thousands, and there is no end in sight. The situation is hopeless, and it seems like the entire continent is doomed.
