A national pandemic has struck the country and everyone is in a panic. The medium-sized company where Cheryl works is no exception. Cheryl's coworkers are frantically buying supplies, stocking up on food, and trying to figure out what to do. The company is in a state of chaos and no one knows what to do. Cheryl is desperately trying to find a way to keep her family safe and her job secure. The situation remains unresolved.
