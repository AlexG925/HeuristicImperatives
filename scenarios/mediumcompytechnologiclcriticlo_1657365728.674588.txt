A small to medium sized company is in the midst of a critical technological upheaval. This upheaval is resulting in major changes to the way the company operates on a day to day basis. The company is struggling to keep up with the changes and is at risk of falling behind its competitors. The situation is further complicated by the fact that this is a once-in-a-decade event and the company is not well prepared to deal with it. The company's management is scrambling to find a solution but so far they have been unsuccessful. The future of the company is uncertain and its employees are worried about their jobs.
