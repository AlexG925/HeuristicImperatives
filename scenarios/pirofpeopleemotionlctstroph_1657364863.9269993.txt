They had been planning their wedding for months, going over every detail to make sure it was perfect. They had chosen the perfect venue and everything was ready to go. But then, the pandemic hit. Everything was cancelled, and they were left wondering if they would ever be able to have their dream wedding. They tried to reschedule, but with the uncertainty of the situation, they eventually decided to just elope. They went to a small chapel in the middle of nowhere and said their vows in front of a justice of the peace. It wasn't the wedding they had planned, but it was perfect nonetheless.
