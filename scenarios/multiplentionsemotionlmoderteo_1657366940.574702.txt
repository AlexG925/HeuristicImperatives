For years, the nations of the world have been at odds with one another. Tensions are high, and there is little trust between the different nations. One day, a small disturbance breaks out between two of the nations. The situation quickly escalates, and soon the entire world is involved in the conflict. The fighting is fierce, and it quickly becomes clear that there is no end in sight. The different nations are evenly matched, and no one is willing to back down. The conflict quickly becomes a stalemate, and the world is plunged into a state of constant warfare. The situation is extremely harrowing for everyone involved. The different nations are constantly at each other's throats, and there is no respite from the fighting. The conflict takes a heavy toll on the people of the world, and it seems like there is no end in sight.
