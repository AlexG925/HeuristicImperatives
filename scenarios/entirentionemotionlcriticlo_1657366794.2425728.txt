The scenario is set in the year 20xx. A group of wealthy elites have been secretly manipulating the government for years, carrying out shady deals and self-serving policies. The people have had enough. They take to the streets in protest, rallying against the government and the wealthy elites. The situation quickly escalates into a full-blown revolution. The government is overthrown and the wealthy elites are forced to flee the country. The people are left to pick up the pieces. The situation is still unresolved and the future is uncertain.
