In the wake of a massive global scandal, the shareholders of XYZ Corporation are in an uproar. The company's stock has plummeted, and its reputation is in shambles. Top executives are scrambling to contain the damage and salvage what they can of the company's reputation. But it may be too late. The public is outraged, and XYZ's once-loyal customers are deserting the company in droves. Worse, the scandal has sparked a wave of class-action lawsuits that could bankrupt the company. XYZ's future is uncertain, and its survival is by no means guaranteed.
