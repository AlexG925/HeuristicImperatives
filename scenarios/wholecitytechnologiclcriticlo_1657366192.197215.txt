A massive earthquake strikes the city, devastating much of the infrastructure. Communications are down, power is out, and water and food are in short supply. Emergency services are overwhelmed, and the city is plunged into chaos. Thousands are dead or injured, and many more are struggling to survive. In the aftermath of the earthquake, the city is a vastly different place. The once-bustling metropolis is now a desolate wasteland, its streets littered with debris and its buildings ruined. But amidst the destruction, there is hope. survivors are slowly beginning to rebuild, and the city is slowly starting to come back to life.
