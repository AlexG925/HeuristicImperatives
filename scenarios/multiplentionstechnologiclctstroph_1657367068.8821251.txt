A massive solar storm known as a Carrington Event hits the earth, knocking out power grids and causing widespread panic and chaos. Within days, the storm has wreaked havoc on the entire planet, leaving entire nations without power or communication. In the aftermath, the United Nations are formed in order to try and resolve the situation, but with so many different nations involved, it quickly becomes clear that there is no easy solution. The situation remains unresolved, and the different nations are left to try and rebuild their societies in the midst of the chaos.
