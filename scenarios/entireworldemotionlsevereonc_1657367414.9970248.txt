It was a dark and stormy night. The wind howled and the rain pelted the windows. The power had been out for hours, and the generators were running low on fuel. The situation was getting desperate. The entire world was in the grip of a severe once-in-a-year uproar. The causes were unknown, but the effects were devastating. crops failed, rivers dried up, and animals died. The human race was struggling to survive. For months, the situation remained unresolved. The world was on the brink of collapse. Then, suddenly, the uproar ended. The rains came, the rivers flowed, and the crops began to grow again. The world was saved, but the damage had been done. The scars of the upheaval would remain for years to come.
