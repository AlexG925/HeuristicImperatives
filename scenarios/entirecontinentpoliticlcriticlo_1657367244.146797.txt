A fictional scenario where an entire continent is in the midst of a critical once-in-a-decade upheaval: The continent is in the midst of a civil war, with various factions fighting for control. The situation is incredibly volatile, and it is unclear who will come out on top. The conflict has engulfed the entire continent, and the death toll is rising. Millions have been displaced, and the humanitarian crisis is worsening by the day. The international community is struggling to find a way to resolve the situation, but so far, all efforts have been unsuccessful. The future of the continent hangs in the balance, and it is uncertain what will happen next.
