The family had just moved into their dream home. It was perfect in every way. But then the unthinkable happened. Their 10-year-old daughter was diagnosed with a rare and aggressive form of cancer. The family was devastated. They had to put their lives on hold to focus on their daughter's treatment. The next few months were a blur of hospital visits, treatment, and anxiety. The family was relieved when their daughter's cancer went into remission. But the happy moment was short-lived. The family was suddenly faced with the possibility of losing their home. The bank was foreclosing on the property. The family was desperate. They had to find a way to save their home. They didn't know what to do.
