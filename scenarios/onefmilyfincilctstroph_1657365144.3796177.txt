The virus is so virulent that it decimates entire families, leaving only a handful of survivors. The Freemans are one of the few families left standing, but they are left with nothing. Their home is ravaged, their possessions are gone, and their loved ones are dead. The only thing they have left is each other. The Freemans are struggling to rebuild their lives. They are grateful to be alive, but they are haunted by their loss. They are also struggling financially. The pandemic has destroyed the economy, and jobs are scarce. The Freemans are doing their best to get by, but they are barely scraping by. They are worried about their future and their ability to provide for their family.
