A small company is in the midst of a severe financial uproar. This is the first time in years that the company has been in such a dire situation and it remains unresolved. The company is struggling to keep its head above water and is forced to make drastic cuts in order to stay afloat. This includes laying off a majority of its workforce, cutting ties with key vendors and suppliers, and cancelling all non-essential projects. The company is in a state of panic as it tries to resolve this situation before it completely sinks.
