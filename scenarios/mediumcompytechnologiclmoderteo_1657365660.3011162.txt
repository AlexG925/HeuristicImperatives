A medium company experiences a moderate once-in-a-month disturbance. The situation remains unresolved. The company's IT department is tasked with investigating the cause of the disturbance and finding a resolution. After a few days of research, they are able to identify the root cause of the issue and put together a plan to fix it. However, before they can implement the fix, the disturbance occurs again, thwarting their efforts. The IT department is now in a race against time to find a resolution before the next disturbance occurs.
