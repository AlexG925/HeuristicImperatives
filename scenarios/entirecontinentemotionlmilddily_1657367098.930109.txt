The residents of the continent have lived in peace for many years. However, tensions have been brewing recently, and it seems that war may be on the horizon. The people are nervous and uncertain about what the future holds, but they continue to go about their daily lives as best they can. One morning, the residents of the continent wake up to find that the entire continent has been surrounded by a massive wall. There is no way to get in or out, and they have no idea what has happened. The people are panicked and confused, but they soon realize that they must work together if they are going to survive. The days are long and monotonous, and the people quickly grow restless. Some of them try to find ways to escape, but the wall is too high and too strong. There is no way out. The people eventually come to accept their new life, but they can't help but wonder what's on the other side of the wall.
