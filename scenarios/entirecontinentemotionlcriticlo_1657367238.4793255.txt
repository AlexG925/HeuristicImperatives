A massive earthquake strikes the continent, causing widespread devastation. Thousands are killed and many more are injured. The scene is one of utter chaos, with buildings collapsing, roads buckling and people desperately trying to flee the carnage. In the aftermath of the disaster, the survivors are left to pick up the pieces. They face a long and difficult road to recovery, with many challenges to overcome. The earthquake has destroyed homes, livelihoods and families. But the survivors are determined to rebuild their lives and their continent.
