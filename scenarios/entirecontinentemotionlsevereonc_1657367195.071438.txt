The survivors of a massive earthquake that struck the continent of Africa are now facing a new danger: a gigantic storm that is quickly approaching. With little time to prepare, everyone is scrambling to find shelter from the incoming storm. The storm is expected to last for days, and with food and water supplies running low, the situation is quickly becoming desperate. In the midst of the chaos, a small group of people are working tirelessly to help those in need. They are going door to door, helping to get people to safety and providing whatever supplies they can find. But they know that they can't do it alone, and they are desperately hoping that the storm will pass quickly so that they can get everyone to safety.
