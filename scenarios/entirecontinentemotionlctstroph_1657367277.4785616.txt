A massive earthquake measuring 9.0 on the Richter scale has struck the continent of Asia, causing widespread devastation. Tens of millions of people have been affected, with over two million confirmed dead. billions of dollars in damage has been inflicted, and entire cities have been leveled. Rescue efforts are ongoing, but the scale of the disaster is so vast that it will take months, if not years, to fully recover. In the meantime, the people of Asia must grapple with the reality that their lives have been forever changed.
