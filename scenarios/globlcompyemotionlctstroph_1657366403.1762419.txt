The world is in a state of pandemonium. A global company has accidentally released a deadly virus into the atmosphere, killing millions of people within weeks. The company is struggling to contain the situation, but the virus is spreading rapidly and there is no known cure. The governments of the world are frantically trying to find a way to stop the virus, but so far they have been unsuccessful. The death toll is rising and the situation is getting more and more desperate. The company is facing lawsuits, protests, and calls for its destruction. It is only a matter of time before the world is plunged into darkness.
