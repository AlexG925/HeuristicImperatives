The year is 2029 and the world is on the brink of political disaster. A new virus has emerged and is spreading quickly, decimating entire families. Panic is setting in as people realize that there is no government response to the crisis. The family at the center of this story is the Smiths. They are a middle-class family living in the suburbs of a major city. They are struggling to survive as the virus claims the lives of their loved ones. The father, John, is a doctor who is working tirelessly to find a cure. The mother, Jane, is trying to keep the family together. The children, Sarah and David, are scared and confused. They are all struggling to cope with the loss of their loved ones and the uncertain future that lies ahead.
