The company's sales have been steadily declining over the past few months, and morale is at an all-time low. The employees are starting to get anxious about their jobs, and there is a lot of tension in the air. One day, a group of employees decide to take matters into their own hands and confront the company's CEO about the situation. The CEO is taken aback by the employees' concerns and promises to look into the matter. However, the situation remains unresolved and the employees are left feeling frustrated and angry.
