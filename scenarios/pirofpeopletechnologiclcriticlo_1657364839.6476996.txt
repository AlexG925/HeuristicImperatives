In a world where technology has advanced to the point where people can upload and download memories, a young couple finds themselves in the midst of a once-in-a-decade upheaval. After a night of passionate lovemaking, they discover that they have accidentally swapped memories. They are unable to go back to their own memories, and must now try to make sense of the other person's life. As they try to piece together the other person's memories, they begin to question their own identities. They also find themselves drawn to each other in ways they never could have imagined. The situation remains unresolved, and they must find a way to live with the knowledge that they have each other's memories.
