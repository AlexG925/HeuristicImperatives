A young woman and her boyfriend are out for a walk near their home when they see a group of people walking towards them carrying signs and chanting. The group is protesting the government's handling of a pandemic that has killed thousands of people and caused widespread panic. The young woman and her boyfriend join the group and march with them to the city center, where they join a larger protest. The government responds by deploying the military to disperse the protesters. The young woman and her boyfriend are both killed in the ensuing violence.
