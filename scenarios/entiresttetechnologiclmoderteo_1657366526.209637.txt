A massive power outage hits the state of California, leaving millions of people without power. The outage is caused by a severe storm that knocks out power lines and downs power poles. The state's power grid is unable to handle the demand, and the power outage lasts for days. People are forced to ration food and water, and some even resort to looting. The state's economy suffers a severe blow, and its infrastructure is badly damaged. The power outage remains unresolved.
