The scenario is set in the present day. A large, global company is in the midst of a financial scandal. This has caused a severe once-in-a-year uproar in the markets, and the situation remains unresolved. The company is facing allegations of fraud, and its stock price has plummeted. The company is in danger of bankruptcy, and its employees are scrambling to find a solution. The future of the company is uncertain, and the situation is causing a great deal of stress for all involved.
