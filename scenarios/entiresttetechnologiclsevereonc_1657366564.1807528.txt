The year is 20xx and the state of California is in the midst of a severe drought. The state's water supply is dwindling and the only way to get water is to buy it from the black market. The price of water is skyrocketing and the state is on the verge of bankruptcy. The governor has declared a state of emergency and the National Guard has been called in to help with the situation. The state is a disaster area and the future is uncertain.
