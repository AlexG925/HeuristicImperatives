The town of Rocksville is in the throes of a critical political upheaval. For the past ten years, the town has been governed by a corrupt mayor and his cronies. The townspeople have had enough of the rampant corruption and have taken to the streets to demand change. The situation has become desperate, with the mayor refusing to step down and the townspeople becoming increasingly violent. The situation is at a standstill, with neither side willing to back down. The town is on the brink of erupting into violence. The mayor has called in the National Guard to quell the unrest, but it is only a matter of time before the situation spirals out of control.
