Alice and Bob are in a relationship. They have been dating for a year and are very happy together. However, every year on their anniversary, a severe argument ensures. It always starts over something small, like who forgot to put the dishes away, and quickly spirals out of control. Things are said that can never be taken back, and the fight always ends with one or both of them in tears. This year, Alice is determined to end the cycle. She's going to confront Bob about the anniversary fight and demand that they finally resolve their issue.
