A powerful storm system blankets the entire continent, unleashing fierce winds and torrential rains. Flooding quickly becomes a major problem, as rivers overflow their banks and flood plains become inundated. mudslides and flash flooding add to the woes of the already beleaguered residents. Power outages are widespread, and communication systems are down. Emergency services are strained to the limit as they respond to countless calls for help. The storm system shows no signs of abating, and the situation looks increasingly dire. In the wake of the storm, the entire continent is left in a state of disarray. Infrastructure has been badly damaged, and many people are without basic necessities like food and water. Rescue and relief efforts are underway, but it will be a long road to recovery. The storm has left a trail of destruction that will take years to heal.
