The scenario is set in a small company with a few hundred employees. The company has been in business for several years and is well-respected in the community. However, every year, there is a major uproar within the company that causes a lot of stress and anxiety for employees. This year, the uproar is over the firing of the company's CEO. The employees are split on whether or not they think the CEO should be fired, and the situation quickly becomes politicized. The employees who support the CEO are angry and upset, while those who think he should be fired are elated and relieved. The situation remains unresolved, and the company is in a state of turmoil.
