A freak storm hits the city, knocking out power and flooding the streets.ade Ruth and John are stuck in their office building, with no way to get home. They're forced to hunker down and ride out the storm together. Ruth and John are forced to work together to survive the storm. They ration their food and water, and take turns standing watch in case of looters or other threats. As the days go by, they grow closer, until they eventually confess their feelings for each other. They share a passionate kiss, just as the power comes back on and they are rescued.
