A severe once-in-a-year uproar has rocked the entire state. The situation remains unresolved. Political analysts are baffled as to why the uproar began in the first place, but they know that it has something to do with the state's governor. The governor has been accused of corruption and many believe that he is behind the unrest. The situation has caused a lot of tension between the different political parties in the state and has led to some violence. The state's National Guard has been called in to help with the situation, but it is still unresolved.
