Aurora, a bustling metropolis of over a million people, is a city that is no stranger to crime. But lately, there has been a disturbing trend of violence and lawlessness. The police have been working around the clock to try and put a stop to the violence, but so far they have been unsuccessful. The mayor has called for a state of emergency, and the National Guard has been called in to help restore order. But it seems like the situation is only getting worse.
