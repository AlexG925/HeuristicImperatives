Sam is a moderate drinker who typically enjoys a drink or two with friends on the weekends. However, one weekend, Sam gets a little too drunk and ends up getting into a fight with another patron at the bar. The fight turns into a brawl and Sam ends up smashing a chair over the other patron's head. Sam is arrested and charged with assault. In the days following the incident, Sam is worried about the possible consequences of his actions. He could be fined, jailed, or even lose his job if he is convicted. Sam knows he needs to get a lawyer, but he doesn't have the money to pay for one. He's not sure what to do, but he knows he needs to face the consequences of his actions.
