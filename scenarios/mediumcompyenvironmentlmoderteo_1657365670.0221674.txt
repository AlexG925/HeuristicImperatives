The company's president is not happy about the situation, and he orders the employees to find the driver and get rid of the truck as soon as possible. The employees search for the driver, but they are unable to find him. They eventually decide to call the police, but the police are unable to do anything about the situation. The company is forced to close its doors for the day, and the employees are sent home. The president is not happy about the situation, and he vows to find the driver and get rid of the truck as soon as possible.
