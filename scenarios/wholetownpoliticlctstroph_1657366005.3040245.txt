A whole town is in panic as a massive, unidentifiable object is headed straight for it. While some people try to evacuate, others are frozen in fear, unsure of what to do. The town mayor tries to take control of the situation and orders everyone to stay calm and stay put. However, as the object gets closer and closer, it becomes clear that it's a massive meteor headed straight for the town. The mayor orders everyone to evacuate immediately, but it's too late. The meteor hits the town, demolishing everything in its path. The town is left in ruins, and the situation remains unresolved.
