The year is 2029 and the world is in the midst of a devastating global pandemic. The disease, which began in Africa, has spread like wildfire across the continent, decimating entire populations. Governments have collapsed, economies have tanked, and society is on the brink of collapse. The few survivors are huddled in makeshift camps, fighting for their lives. In the midst of this chaos, a new leader has emerged. A charismatic politician who has promised to restore order and bring the continent back from the brink. She has quickly gained a following of millions of desperate people. But not everyone is convinced. There are those who believe she is a fraud, a charlatan who will only make the situation worse. As the two sides prepare for a final showdown, the future of the continent hangs in the balance.
