A small company is thrust into the middle of a political nightmare when a catastrophic event strikes their city. With the government in disarray and the situation quickly spiraling out of control, the company is forced to take matters into their own hands to ensure their survival. As the days go by, it becomes increasingly clear that the company is the only thing keeping the city from descending into complete chaos. With food and supplies running low, and tensions running high, it won't be long before the company is forced to make some tough choices. Can they continue to protect their employees and shareholders, or will they be forced to abandon their values in order to survive?
