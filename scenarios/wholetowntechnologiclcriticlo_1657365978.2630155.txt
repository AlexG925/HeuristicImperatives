A powerful earthquake shakes the small town of Roseville to its core. The damage is severe, with many buildings completely destroyed and others left in a state of disrepair. The town's residents are left scrambling to find shelter and basic necessities. With power and communication lines down, the town is effectively cut off from the outside world. Help is unlikely to arrive anytime soon. The town's residents must band together and use their own resources to rebuild their homes and their lives.
