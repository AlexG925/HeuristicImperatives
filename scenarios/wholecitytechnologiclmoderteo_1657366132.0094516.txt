A mysterious pulse has hit the city, disrupting all technology. For a few days, everything is in disarray as the pulse knocks out power grids, communication systems, and transportation. People are forced to rely on old-fashioned methods of getting around and communicating with each other. Life returns to a slower pace, without the constant hustle and bustle of technology. As the days go by, it becomes apparent that the pulse is not going away. It seems to be permanent, and the city must learn to adapt. New systems are put in place to deal with the lack of technology, and people find ways to go about their lives without it. The city becomes a calmer place, without the constant noise and distraction of technology.
